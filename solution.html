Our Solution

Our solution to reducing this problem is to create a website and an app that allows the user to identify and calculate how much Carbon dioxide (Co2) they consume in their daily lives. They can calculate and get an estimation by using the survey that we have created. In this survey they will be questioned with questions like “how many times do you eat meat?” and “how many times do you use your car to work” and many more. When you finish up this survey you will get an estimation of your personal Co2 emission. We want to focus on the app because according to pewresearch they are estimating that there are around 5 billion people who does have a cellphone and half off these own a smartphone. In the areas where the smart phone isn’t available then the website comes into play. By other words we want to make this survey accessible for everyone so everybody could be capable of contributing for change.
